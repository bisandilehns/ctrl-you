Tips & Strategies

Set App Limits

Use built-in screen time tools to set daily limits for apps that take up too much of your time. Adjust these limits weekly based on your usage to stay balanced without cutting out essential apps.

No Phone in Bed

Charge your phone outside the bedroom to avoid late-night scrolling and improve your sleep quality. Try keeping a notebook nearby for any late-night ideas instead of reaching for your phone.

Replace Scrolling

Keep a book, puzzle, or journal nearby so you have a fun alternative when youâ€™re bored. Even small hobbies like sketching or listening to music can break the scrolling habit.

Tech-Free Breaks

Take short walks, stretch, or do breathing exercises without your phone. Even 5-10 minutes of focused relaxation can boost your mood and productivity.

Track Your Progress

Check your screen time weekly and celebrate small improvements. Reflect on what triggers extra screen time and plan strategies to reduce it gradually.
